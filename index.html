Why we built this

The ancient Agora was Athens' public square, a single open space where philosophers, merchants, politicians, and citizens could engage in discourse without an agenda. Socrates did not have a newspaper. Aristotle did not run a podcast. Plato wasn't part of a PAC. They stood in an open space and engaged, and in the end, the best arguments survived.

We are not under the illusion that our group conducts ourselves in the same way as the great philosophers and the Ancient Greeks, but we do think something essential has been lost in the way ideas circulate today.

In an era full of noise, monetization, and clickbait, we have forgotten the slow, careful, adversarial working through of a question to its logical conclusion.

Now more than ever, thoughtfulness, analysis, and truth must prevail.

"It is better to change an opinion than to persist in a wrong one."

— Socrates

What we cover

Finance, economics, business, politics, philosophy, and the occasional piece that refuses to fit any of those categories. What ties them together is a commitment to analysis over opinion, and to first principles over received wisdom. We are not interested in hot takes. We are interested in whether or not the reasoning holds.

How we write

Our team does not have a house view. Disagreement is a part of our identity, not a problem. Our only agenda is letting knowledge and the best ideas prevail.

We are not selling anything because the truth cannot be bought.

We cite our sources, admit openly when we do not know something, grow when we are proven wrong, and stand firmly with our own names in the byline. Anonymous analysis is cowardly analysis.

AI Policy

At Agora, we take immense pride in the pieces we publish, because our pieces are our own. All pieces published on this site are solely written by our authors and always will be. If we let Artificial Intelligence write to what they perceive as the truth, then we risk giving it up completely, especially to those at the top.

However, we are aware that in the modern era, there can be almost no progression in the tech realm without the use of AI. This is why we are transparent in the fact that AI is utilized in the development of this website, as well as in occasional research and data organization.

Always as a tool to enhance our work, but never to replace it.
