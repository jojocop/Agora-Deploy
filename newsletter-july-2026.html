NexGen Energy (NYSE: NXE)
BUY: the West's largest undeveloped uranium asset enters construction.
Current Price
US$9.25
Our Target
US$13
Horizon
12–18 mo

The business got stronger in 2026. The stock fell 30% anyway. In March, NexGen received the final licence for Rook I in Saskatchewan — ending twelve years of permitting risk on ore 20 to 30 times richer than the global average — while uranium contract prices sit at 18-year highs near US$93 a pound.

Cash costs near US$10 a pound, payback in roughly a year, and first production timed into the widest projected supply deficit on record. The market is still trading the spot chart. We think that gap is the entry point — the financing package due by early 2027 is the catalyst to watch.
